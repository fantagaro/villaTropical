LOCATION

Noord, Aruba

The weather on Aruba is perfect year-round, with high temperatures averaging 82º F and frequent trade winds cooling your relaxation in the shade. The island's unique location in the Southwestern Caribbean places it well south of the hurricane belt, ensuring a safe and comfortable stay in any season.

Noord is the center of the action on the island, with many of Aruba's finest beaches, dining, and activities. White sand Eagle Beach and Palm Beach, five of the island's top-10 restaurants, and one of the world's best windsurfing spots are all nearby.

Villa Tropical commands nearly three acres atop a hill overlooking Noord. The home's accommodations, amenities, and gardens are described by many who know Aruba as some of the finest on the island.

FAQ

FAQ

These are some of the most frequently asked questions about the property. If you have a question that is not answered here, please feel free to contact us.

Q How do I reach Villa Tropical on arrival?

The villa is a short 15 minutes from Queen Beatrix International Airport. Click here for driving directions.

We recommend renting a car for the duration of your stay, so that you can easily reach all of the restaurants, activities, and beaches on the island. Rentals are easily arranged from Queen Beatrix International Airport.

The address is Boegoeroei 33C (pronounced boo-goo-roo-i), Noord. When arriving in Noord, you will see a stoplight at the local police station. Take the road to downtown. You will pass a church on your left. On your right, you will see a Direct TV office. Turn right on the road just past here. You will then pass Fung Lam Market on the right. Continue up this road. At the top of the hill you will arrive at the villa, straight ahead.

Q How far away is the beach?

The closest beaches are Eagle Beach and Palm Beach. Both can be seen from the rear of the property, and are a short 3-minute drive away.

Q Can we pick the fruit from the trees?

Yes! You may take as much as you can use. Javier, our property manager, can help you with the coconuts. (He also makes a tasty lemonade.)

Q How do I arrange for spa services?

Javier and Lerita are your personal concierges. Their phone numbers will be provided when you arrive. Please also see the spa menu. It is best to make arrangements with them prior to your arrival to guarantee what services you may request. We recommend the 90 minute couples massage, followed by a pedicure. Your concierges can also make other arrangements for you, including dinner reservations, sailboat trips, diving, or nearly anything else you may need on the island.

Q What is your cancellation policy?

All rentals are pre-paid and non-refundable. We recommend purchasing travelers insurance, a very affordable option for any unexpected changes in your travel plans.

Q What types of power plugs are used in Aruba?

Two-prong 110-120 Volt Grounded and Non-Grounded plugs, the same as you would find in North America and Japan. Devices from North America and Japan will charge without issue. For devices from other parts of the world, you may need to bring adapters or voltage transformers.
